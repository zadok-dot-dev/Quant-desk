Position sizing

Edge = True P − Market Price. Kelly stake uses f* = (P − price) / (1 − price), scaled by your Kelly fraction. Contracts sharing a correlation group get their stake divided by the number of contracts in that group (Phase 3 haircut) — a conservative approximation, not a full covariance model. Max buy price = True P minus your buffer: the price above which the edge you priced in no longer exists.

What this is

A sizing and record-keeping tool for the workflow you described. It does the math (Phases 3–4: correlation haircut, Quarter-Kelly stake, max buy price) and keeps a persistent log of what you actually bet and how it turned out. It does not pull live stats or live market prices — see the full doc for exactly what's covered and what isn't.
